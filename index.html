Shubham Tidke (born 2006) is an Indian computer science student and software developer specializing in front-end development and data science. Currently pursuing a degree in SE Computer Engineering, he is recognized for his work in modern web technologies and data-driven solutions.

At the age of 19, Tidke has demonstrated proficiency in multiple programming languages and frameworks, with a particular focus on creating responsive, user-friendly web applications. His work spans across various domains including web development, data analysis, and machine learning applications.
